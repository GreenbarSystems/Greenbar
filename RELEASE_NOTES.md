## What's new

Greenbar's first public release. A private, offline-first personal-finance
app that turns your bank statements into clear money decisions — without
ever sending your data anywhere.

### The big picture
- **100% on-device.** No accounts. No cloud. No servers. Your transactions,
  budgets, and goals never leave your phone.
- **CSV or PDF import.** Bring statements in from any bank; Greenbar parses
  them locally.
- **Encrypted backup + restore.** You control the file, you control the
  passphrase.
- **PIN + biometric unlock** for privacy-by-default.

### Everything in this release
- Spending summary with period switcher (Current month / Last 3 months / YTD)
  and collapsible breakdowns.
- Tracker (budget) with category targets and variance hero.
- Transactions tab with the import-trust bar so you can see where every
  number came from.
- Anomaly detection, recurring-charge detection, and goal tracking.
- 3-step monthly checkup routine.
- Reconciliation: match imported totals against your statement.
- "How Greenbar works" tour accessible any time from Settings.

## Known limitations
- iCloud Drive / Google Drive backups are user-initiated only — there is no
  automatic cloud sync (by design).
- A small number of bank exports use non-standard column names; you can
  remap them in **Settings → Import & Accounts → Format & region**.
